Learn how to make this ferocious origami lion with just a few simple folds.

Step 1: Start with a square sheet of origami paper, color side down. If you only have regular rectangular paper, follow instructions on how to make origami paper.

Step 2: Fold the left half to the right, corner to corner, then unfold.

Step 3: Fold the top half down, then unfold again.

Step 4: Fold a part of the bottom corner up as shown in the picture.

Step 5: Fold the left and right corners in above the horizontal crease like in the picture.

Step 6: Turn the paper over horizontally.

Step 7: Fold down the top part, beginning at slightly below the halfway crease.

Step 8: Turn the figure over.

Step 9: Draw on the face.
