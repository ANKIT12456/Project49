Learn how to make this cute origami rabbit with just a few simple folds.

Step 1: Start with a square piece of origami paper with white side up. If you only have regular 8.5x11 paper, follow these instructions to make it into a square sheet.

Step 2: Fold the paper in half by folding the bottom corner to the top corner, then unfold.

Step 3: Now fold the left corner to the right corner, then unfold.

Step 4: Fold a small portion of the triangle base up as shown.

Step 5: Fold the left and right bottom edges up at the center as shown. These are the origami rabbit ears.

Step 6: Turn the figure over.

Step 7: Fold the top corner of the head down as shown.

Step 8: Fold the bottom corner of the chin up as shown, then draw on the face and you're done.
